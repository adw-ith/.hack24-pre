Sustainable Development Goals

Good health and well-being

In this theme, they can build a medical alert system to call up an ambulance when in a medical emergency alone or an app to track our workout sessions are ways to safeguard one’s life and health.

Quality Education

Under this theme, participants develop their projects for advancing education to a wide range of communities. New types of innovation in the classroom system and distance learning etc. can be developed.

Affordable and clean energy

Moving to a more energy-consuming generation we need more efficient use of energy to tackle the energy crisis. Participants do show a keen interest in this field and have been successful in developing new ways to save energy.

Industry, Innovation and infrastructure

Under this theme participants have opportunities to build systems for various Industries, Innovative projects and also new infrastructure models for various needs in this rapidly changing world

Sustainable Cities and Communities

Smart and planned city with more facilities with a clean and green environment. Projects aiming for the development of these kinds of activities will come under this section.

Life on Land

Life on earth is becoming harsher with changing climate and unpredictable weather changes, whether it is a way to cool the house or an app to track climate change all incorporate technology and skilled people in its backend.
